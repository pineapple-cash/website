Pineapple Cash

Pineapple Cash is the future of all mining cryptocurrencies, for those that are seeking a green and energy efficient method to have tokens farmed.

Read our YellowPaper

Produced by Farming

Pineapple Cash can only be created by farming the seed token called Nitrogen. Farming requires 90 days before you can harvest.

Planted Tokens Burned

All Nitrogen seed tokens that have been planted are burned and can not be recovered. Reducing the total circulating supply.

Limited in Supply

Pineapple Cash is limited to a max supply of 500,000,000 as the maximum which can ever be farmed from Nitrogen.

Pineapple Cash Ecosystem

Mining Reinvented. Gamification.

Fun and engaging. KISS principle.

Liquidity Locked

We have locked 100% of the total supply of Nitrogen to PancakeSwap, and burned the keys. Verify burn here.

Tokens Burned

As you use your Nitrogen to grow Pineapple Cash all tokens used in production are burned and are non recoverable.

Finite Max Supply

Only 500M Pineapple Cash can ever be produced from farming. There are only 50T Nitrogen tokens for farming.

Farming Pineapple Cash

Requires exactly 100k Nitrogen tokens and a farming period of 90 days to produce 1 Pineapple Cash token.

100% Decentralized

Farming

How to farm.

Our farming algorithm is a first of its kind, the only
cryptocurrency to be produced from equipmentless farming.

The Catlast

The token Nitrogen (NITRO) is needed to grow Pineapple Cash (PNAPL).

Growing Process

A staking / farming period of 90 days considered Proof of Time (PoT) algorithm.

Token Burn

All Nitrogen (NITRO) used in the farming process is burned once finished and gone forever.

Token Harvest

At the end of the 90 day farming period you are rewarded with 1 Pineapple Cash (PNAPL).
